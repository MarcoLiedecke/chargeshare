About ChargeShare

ChargeShare is revolutionizing EV charging by connecting private charger owners with drivers who need to charge their vehicles.

Founded in 2025, our mission is to expand the charging infrastructure by utilizing existing private charging stations more efficiently, creating a win-win situation for both parties.

Our Vision

A world where EV charging is accessible everywhere and benefits everyone.

Our Mission

To connect EV drivers with available chargers while helping charger owners earn passive income.

Our Story

The Idea

It all started when our founder couldn't find a charging station during a road trip, despite passing many homes with private chargers. This sparked the idea: what if these private chargers could be shared?

Launch

After months of development, ChargeShare was launched in early 2025. Our platform quickly gained traction as both EV owners and charger owners saw the value in our sharing model.

Growth

Today, we're expanding rapidly across cities, with hundreds of private chargers becoming part of our network. We're building the most convenient and sustainable charging infrastructure together.
