Share Your Charger, Earn Money

ChargeShare helps EV owners make money from their private chargers when they're not in use, while helping other EV drivers find convenient charging options.

Get Started

Key Features

Find Nearby Chargers

Easily locate available charging stations on our interactive map. Filter by charger type, power output, and availability.

Earn From Your Charger

Set your own prices and availability. Make your charger work for you when you're not using it.

Secure & Reliable

Verified users, secure payments, and reliable ratings ensure a trustworthy experience for everyone.

How It Works

For Charger Owners

  1. Register your account and add your charger details
  2. Set your availability and hourly rate
  3. Approve reservation requests
  4. Provide access to your charger
  5. Get paid automatically

For EV Drivers

  1. Create an account and add payment method
  2. Find available chargers near you
  3. Book a charging slot
  4. Arrive and charge your vehicle
  5. Rate your experience
